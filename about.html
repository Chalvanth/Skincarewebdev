FOUNDING STORY

BEST FRIENDS. NATURE LOVERS. SKIN CARE ENTHUSIASTS

Just like any best friends, we grew up sharing our dreams and aspirations and skincare was something we were most excited about. The markets are crowded with skincare products that claim to work wonders on all types of skin.

A deeper look at these products made us realise how ineffective they could be on Indian skin. From diverse climatic conditions to high levels of pollution, and varying psychological factors, there are diverse factors that take a toll on the Indian skin. As we proceeded deeper into the subject, we got to know how most people start experiencing skin-related issues after 25. We understood the role of the dropping collagen levels and how chemicals gave instant results but left long term harm.

With enough knowledge, we decided to put our Bachelors’ Degree in Biotechnology to good useto redefine the raw state of the skincare industry in India. With this philosophy, we started exploring ingredients with the aim of ingraining nature with science and our hunt took us to naturally occurring peptides and their ability to nullify the harm caused by age, pollution and chemical treatments.

Holding on to the concept of “skin intelligence”, we pioneered natural skincare because “what you apply on the skin is as important as what goes into it”, yet, there’s the challenge of finding products with “science” and “natural” together. Our formulations offer the most skin-friendly solution to fill in this gap.

With natural ingredients extracted from Indian flowers, plants, seeds, nuts and herbs, we aim at offering you powerhouse products that make skincare a soiree.
